Lasagna

Best Lasagna in the World

Quick and easy lasagna made with easy to find ingredients.

Ingredients:
  • Ground Beef and Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
Lasagna Steps:
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375 degrees for 50 minutes.
  6. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
